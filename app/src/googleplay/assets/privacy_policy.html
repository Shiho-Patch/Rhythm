Privacy Policy for Rhythm Music Player

Last Updated: January 19, 2026 | Google Play Edition

Privacy-First Approach: Rhythm is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.
Note: This is the Google Play Store version of Rhythm, which uses only officially supported APIs for enhanced compliance.

1. Introduction

Rhythm Music Player ("we," "our," or "the app") is a free, open-source music player for Android available on the Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles data when you use it.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Rhythm does not collect or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising ID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Listening history or playback statistics
  • Contact information
  • Biometric data
  • Financial information
  • Any other personally identifiable information

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All app data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Music Library: The app scans your device's local music files to create a playable library
  • Playback Statistics: Play counts, last played times, and favorites are stored locally in a private app database
  • App Settings: Your preferences (theme, equalizer settings, etc.) are saved in device storage
  • Playlists: Custom playlists you create are stored only on your device
  • Downloaded Artwork: Album covers and artist images are cached locally in the app's cache directory
  • Lyrics: Downloaded synchronized lyrics are stored in the app's private storage

4. Internet Access and Third-Party Services

Rhythm may use internet connectivity for optional features. All online features are disabled by default and require your explicit opt-in through app settings.

4.1 Optional Online Features (User Configurable)

The Google Play version includes the following optional online services:

  • Deezer API: To fetch high-quality album artwork and artist images using Deezer's public API
  • LRCLib: To download synchronized lyrics for songs from the open-source LRCLib service
  • Spotify Search API: For enhanced metadata search (requires user to provide their own Spotify developer credentials)

4.2 Data Sent to Third-Party Services

When you enable online features, only the following minimal, non-personal data is sent:

  • Song Metadata: Artist name, song title, album name (for artwork/lyrics lookup only)
  • HTTP Headers: Standard browser headers (User-Agent, Accept)

Important:

  • No device identifiers are sent
  • No user profiles or accounts are created
  • No listening habits are tracked or shared
  • All API calls are made anonymously
  • You can disable these features at any time in Settings

4.3 Third-Party Privacy Policies

When you use optional online features, the following third-party services may be contacted:

  • Deezer API: Deezer Privacy Policy
  • LRCLib: Open-source lyrics service (lrclib.net) - No tracking, no registration
  • Spotify API: Spotify Privacy Policy (only if you configure your own API credentials)

We do not control these third-party services and are not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. Permissions Used by the App

Rhythm requests the following Android permissions for core functionality:

  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO (Android 13+) / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To access and play your local music files
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To save downloaded artwork (Android 9 and below only)
  • INTERNET: For optional online features (artwork, lyrics) - disabled by default
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check if network is available before making API calls
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To play music in the background with a notification
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): To display playback control notifications
  • WAKE_LOCK: To keep music playing when screen is off

Permission Justification: All permissions are used solely for app functionality (music playback, artwork display, background operation) and not for data collection, tracking, or advertising.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data to share.

  • No data is sold to advertisers
  • No analytics companies receive your information
  • No data brokers have access to app data
  • The app contains no advertising SDKs
  • The app contains no analytics or tracking libraries

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Rhythm does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app:

  • Does not require any account creation
  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not include social features or chat
  • Does not share data with third parties

Parents can safely allow children to use Rhythm without privacy concerns.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not maintain servers or databases with user data
  • Your data cannot be breached through our systems
  • Data security depends on your device's security (PIN, password, biometric lock)
  • We recommend keeping your Android OS updated for best security

9. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Uninstalling the app: Removes all app data from your device
  • Clearing app data: Deletes all settings, playlists, and cached artwork
  • Cache clearing: Removes downloaded artwork and lyrics while keeping settings

You have full control over your data at all times.

  • Right to Access: Not applicable (no data collected)
  • Right to Deletion: Simply uninstall the app
  • Right to Portability: All data remains on your device
  • Right to Opt-Out: Online features are opt-in only

13. California Residents (CCPA)

  • Sell personal information

14. European Users (GDPR)
